Stables Cottage

An 18th-century former stables on the border of Lainshaw Woods, Stewarton. Purchased for £190,000 in 2020, carefully renovated for £40,000, and sold for £250,000 in 2023.

New herringbone flooring throughout, freestanding bath, built-in wardrobes, full redecoration, and a three-level landscaped garden designed to complement the woodland setting.
