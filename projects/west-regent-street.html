West Regent Street

A top-floor one-bedroom apartment in De Quincey House, Glasgow — an Alfred Waterhouse building originally built for the Prudential insurance company. Purchased for £115,000 in 2017 and renovated for £18,500, featuring alcove shelving, a kitchen peninsula, Japanese soaking tub, and new lighting circuits throughout. Sold for £180,000 in 2019.
