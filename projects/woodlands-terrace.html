Woodlands Terrace

A two-bedroom top-floor flat in a Glasgow townhouse. Purchased for £272,500 in 2023 and comprehensively renovated for £75,000. The property had an awkward layout and bore signs of its past as commercial premises — transformed into a colourful, characterful home.

New kitchen with Everhot electric stove, reconfigured bedrooms and bathroom, Ventrolla window refurbishment, new engineered wood flooring, and full redecoration throughout.
