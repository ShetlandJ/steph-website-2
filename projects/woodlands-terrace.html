Woodlands Terrace

Two-bedroom terraced flat, top floor townhouse

Woodlands Terrace - Hero Image Placeholder

Project Details

  • Property Type: Two-bedroom terraced flat
  • Location: Top floor of townhouse
  • Purchase Price: £272,500 (2023)
  • Renovation Cost: £75,000
  • Status: For sale (2025)

Major Improvements

  • Window refurbishment by Ventrolla
  • New boiler installation
  • Complete kitchen redesign
  • Bathroom and bedroom reconfiguration
  • New engineered wood flooring
  • Built-in storage solutions

Project Story

Woodlands Terrace, a two-bedroom terraced flat on the top floor of a townhouse, had an awkward layout and still bore signs of its past as a commercial premises. This comprehensive renovation transformed the space into a modern, functional home while respecting its character.

Renovation Programme

Structural & Systems

  • Window refurbishment by Ventrolla
  • New boiler installation
  • New radiators throughout

Kitchen & Living

  • A new kitchen and the reinstatement of a chimney alcove for an electric stove
  • New engineered wood flooring and carpets throughout
  • Installation of a utility room

Bedrooms & Bathrooms

  • Reconfigured the layout of the bathroom and both bedrooms
  • Incorporating a storage loft and increasing the size of both bedrooms
  • Reinstating cornicing in new rooms to match original

Storage & Details

  • Built-in shelving/cupboard for cat litter tray in the hall
  • Full redecoration and new light fittings throughout
