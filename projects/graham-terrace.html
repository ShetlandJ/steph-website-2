Graham Terrace

Stewarton - Top-floor flat in terraced house

Graham Terrace - Hero Image Placeholder

Project Details

  • Property Type: Top-floor flat
  • Location: Terraced house, Stewarton
  • Purchase Price: £80,000
  • Renovation Cost: £11,500
  • Sale Price: £97,562
  • Capital Growth: £17,562

Key Improvements

  • Kitchen renovation
  • Wall removal for open plan
  • Corner utility room addition
  • Pocket door installation
  • Boiler replacement
  • Space optimization

Project Story

A top-floor flat in a terraced house in Stewarton. This flat was previously renovated to form three bedrooms instead of two, and was in good condition. Our renovation focused on efficient improvements that would maximize the living space and add value through smart design decisions.

Renovation Programme

Kitchen & Living Integration

  • Kitchen completely renovated
  • Wall between kitchen and living room removed
  • Open plan living created for better flow and light
  • Space planning to maximize functionality

Utility & Storage Solutions

  • Small corner utility room built
  • Pocket door installed to save space
  • Boiler and washer/dryer housed in utility room
  • Clever storage solutions implemented

Systems Upgrade

  • Boiler replaced for improved efficiency
  • Utilities reorganized for better functionality

Design Philosophy

This project demonstrated how strategic improvements can significantly enhance both the functionality and value of a property without extensive renovation. The key was identifying the right interventions that would have maximum impact.

The removal of the wall between the kitchen and living room transformed the feel of the flat, creating a sense of space and light that belied the compact footprint. The corner utility room with pocket door was a masterstroke of space planning, hiding necessary appliances while maintaining the clean lines of the open plan area.

Efficient Investment

With a renovation cost of just £11,500, this project achieved excellent value for money. The £17,562 capital appreciation demonstrates how targeted improvements can deliver strong returns when executed thoughtfully.

The project showed our ability to work within tight budgets while still delivering transformative results. Every pound spent was carefully considered to ensure maximum impact on both livability and value.

Space Planning Excellence

The pocket door solution for the utility room exemplifies our attention to detail in space planning. In a compact flat, every square foot matters, and the ability to hide the boiler and washing appliances without sacrificing accessibility was crucial to the project's success.
