Project Details

  • Property Type: Two-bedroom flat
  • Location: Camberwell Grove, London
  • Purchase Price: £500,000
  • Renovation Cost: £30,000
  • Final Valuation: £600,000
  • Project Duration: May/June 2025

Key Improvements

  • Complete redecoration throughout
  • All flooring replaced
  • New light fixtures installed
  • New shower screen fitted
  • Front door replacement
  • Kitchen worktop and tiling renewal

Project Story

A two-bedroom flat in the Camberwell Grove conservation area of London, I renovated this property in preparation for sale in May/June 2025. Initially valued at £400-425k, it was re-valued at £600k at time of listing in July.

Full redecoration was required, including replacement of all flooring, re-painting, new light fixtures and a new shower screen. The front door was replaced, the kitchen worktop and tiling was re-done and built-in wardrobes were demolished and replaced with modern doors.

The exterior windows were fully restored and repainted and hardware throughout the flat was replaced including window and door furniture.

"You know when you get a really drastic haircut and you keep catching yourself in the mirror and going "who is that?? Oooh!"? Well it's like that a hundred times. I keep finding little touches and changes. Well done!!"

Grove Park Client
